Bonjour Gallery
BONJOUR Gallery is a gallery in which visitors of all ages journey through major events in art history. Visitors enter this area of the interactive gallery through a realistic cave and are able to view early Paleolithic drawings on the cave walls. Upon exiting the cave, guests move into ancient cultures like Mesopotamia, Egypt and Greece, and finally, the exhibit ends in the modern-age of art.
